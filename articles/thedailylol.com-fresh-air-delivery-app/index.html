Startup creates app that delivers fresh air to your doorstep

April 8, 2023

A new startup called FreshAir has recently launched an app that aims to deliver fresh air to your doorstep, making it easier than ever to breathe clean and crisp air, even in polluted cities.

How does it work?

FreshAir collects air from pristine environments such as remote forests, mountain tops, and coastal regions. The air is then packaged in eco-friendly, recyclable containers and shipped to customers. Using the FreshAir app, customers can choose the type of air they want to receive, with options ranging from alpine to coastal air. The app also allows users to set up a subscription plan for regular deliveries.

Why is this app necessary?

With growing concerns about air pollution and its effects on health, FreshAir aims to provide a solution for individuals living in areas with poor air quality. According to the World Health Organization, 9 out of 10 people worldwide breathe polluted air, which is responsible for approximately 7 million premature deaths each year. FreshAir's mission is to improve the health and well-being of its customers by offering a convenient source of fresh air.

How has the app been received?

Since its launch, the FreshAir app has been met with mixed reviews. Some users have praised the innovative concept, claiming that it has improved their quality of life, while others have criticized the app as a marketing gimmick. Nevertheless, FreshAir has managed to attract a substantial number of subscribers and continues to grow in popularity.

Future developments

FreshAir plans to expand its services by partnering with local governments to install air purification systems in public spaces.
